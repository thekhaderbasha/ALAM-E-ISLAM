ALAM-E-ISLAM
namza ( salah )
before namaz

step 1

jagah saaf sudhara hai ya nai ek baar dekle, aur apka jism aur kapade aur namaz ke jagah saaf hai ya nai dekle

step 2

wuzu karo. namaz shuroo karane se pahale aap paak saaf hona jaroori hai.

wuzu ka tareeqa

step 3

qible ke taraf muh karke kade, sabhee musalamaan apanee namaaz ada karte waqt har din may paanch baar kaaba ka saamana karte hain.

step 4

sahi waqt par namaz adha kare.har din 5 namaz ka ek sahi waqt hota hai, usi samay may namaz adha kare, kabi kabi namaz ke waqt may 5 ya 10 min ka farak hota hai

namaz ka waqt

step 5

namaz say phele apne jism ko poori tharah say chupaye jisa kapde phene, mard aur aurat namaz say phele apne jism ko chupe jisa kapdo ko phen ne chahiye, aurat surat aur haat ke siwa apna jism chupe jisa kapade phene , aur mard apne kambar say leke paav ka takno thak chupe jisa kapade phene

namaz ka tareeqa

Step 1

Pehlay niyyat karien

(jitne rakat aap padna chahte hai [2,3,4]) rakaat (jis waqt ke namaz padne wale hai FAJAR,JPHAR,ASAR,MAGREEB,ISHA) namaaz ki ada karta/karti hoon, Allaah kay waastay muuh mera kaaba shareef ki taraf

ex: fajar ke 2 rakat faraj namaz adha karta/karti hu, allah ke vaste muuh mera kaaba shareef ki taraf

step 2

Phir TAKBEER kahein : Allaahu akbar

apne dono haaton ko kaano thak leke jaiye

step 3

1. apane haathon ko neeche karen aur unhen apanee naabhi ke oopar rakhen. aapaka seedha(right) haath aapake baen(left) haath ke oopar rakha kar

2. apanee aankhon ko apane saamane jameen par kendrit rakhen.
paigambar muhammad (sallallaahu alaihi va sallam) ne kaha ki jab aap namaz padte hain to apanee nigah ko hamesha sajda karne jagah rakni chahiye.

3.haalaanki yah jaroori nahin hai, phir bhee allaahu akabar kahane ke baad neeche ke dua (praarthana) ko padhane kee atyadhik anushansa kee jaatee hai

sana:

" subhanakal-lahumma wabihamdika
watabarakas-muka
wataa ‘ala jadduka
wala ilaha ghayruka."

meaning: Ya Allah! Main Teri Paki Bayan Karta Hun Aur Teri Hamd (Taareef) Wa Sana Bayan Karta Hun Aur Tera Naam Barkat Wala Hai, Buland Hai Teri Shaan, Aur Nahi Hai Maabood Tere Siva Koi.

step 4

quran-e-shareef ka phele surah pade (surah-fateha)

A’auodu billaahi minash-shaytaanir rajeem
Bismillaahir ar-Rahmani ar-Raheem

Al hamdu lillaahi rabbil ‘alameen
Ar-Rahmani ar-Raheem
Maaliki yawmid deen
Iyyaaka na’aboodu wa iyyaaka nasta’een
Ihdeenas siraatal mustaqeem
Siraatal ladheena an ‘amta’ alayhim
Ghayril maghduubi’ alayhim waladawleen.

soorah fateha padne ke baad, aap quraan se ek aur surah jaise soorah ikhalaas pade ya koi aur surah. haalaanki, kam se kam aapako namaaz kee har rakat mein soorah faatiha padhana chaahie.

step 5

ruku may jaiye

jaise hee aap jhuk rahe hain allaahu akabar kahen.
apanee peeth ko seedha rakh kar ruku karen, apane haathon ko apane ghutanon par, aur apanee nigaho ko us jameen par kendrit karen jahaan aap sajda karenge.

jab aap ruku mein hon to aap is dua ko teen baar pado:

"Subhanna rabbeeyal adheem"

meaning:mera allah sabase mahaan hai, sabse meharbaan Hai

step 6

ab wapas seedha khade hojaiye

jaise hee aap rukoo se uthakar khade honge , aap apane haathon ko apane kaanon ke paas tak le aur kahe:

"Samee Allahu leeman hameeda"

meaning:"allaah unakee sunata hai jo usakee taareeph karate hain"

aur jab aap seedhe khade hon to apane haathon ko kamar se neeche kar len aur kahen:

"Rabbana walakal hamd"

meaning:" aye mere parwardhigaar! aap he ke waste tamaam"

step 7

ab sajda karen

jaise hee aap sajda kar rahe honge , allaahu akabar kahen aur sajda karen

jab aap sajde may honge, aapke naak, donon haathon kee hatheliyaan, ghutane aur donon pair kee ungaliyaan jameen ko chuna chahiye ya lage rahna chahiye.

jab aap sajde may ho isko 3 baar pade:

"Subhanna rabbeeyal ‘alaa"

meaning:paaki bayan karta hu my mere parwardeghaar ke jo badi bulandi wala hai

step 8

sujud se utho aur ek pal baitho

sujud se uthate hee allaahu akabar bolen aur phir apane baen pair par baith jaen. aapaka baayaan pair pharsh par tika rahega jabaki aapaka daahina pair seedha hoga. apane haathon ko apane ghutanon par rakhen.

jab aap is sthiti mein hon to allaah se maafi maangane kee salaah dee jaatee hai. aasaan dua aap kah sakate hain

"Rabbigh-fir lee"

meaning:‘aye paak parvardighar muje maaf karde mere gunahon ko maaf karde

step 9

ab phir sajda karen

jaise hee aap sajda kar rahe honge , allaahu akabar kahen aur sajda karen

jab aap sajde may honge, aapke naak, donon haathon kee hatheliyaan, ghutane aur donon pair kee ungaliyaan jameen ko chuna chahiye ya lage rahna chahiye.

jab aap sajde may ho isko 3 baar pade:

"Subhanna rabbeeyal ‘alaa"

meaning:paaki bayan karta hu my mere parwardeghaar ke jo badi bulandi wala hai

step 10

sujde se uthakar khade hone kee sthiti mein lautana aur allaahu akabar kahana

alhamdulillah , aapane abhee-abhee ek pooree rakat pooree kee hai! har namaaz kee pahalee rakat in 10 charanon ke saath kee jaatee hai.

"At Tahiyyaatu lilaahi was Salawaatu wat tayibaatu
Assalaamu ‘alaika ayyuhan nabiyyu wa rahmatu Allahi wa barakaatuh
Assalaamu ‘alaynaa wa ‘alaa ‘ebaadillaahis saaliheen,
Ash hadu allaa ilaha illa Allah Wa ash hadu anna Muhammadan ‘abduhuu wa rasuuluh
"

meaning:saare salaam allaah ke lie hai, aur sabhee praarthanaen (namaaz) aur bhalaeeyaan. aman(shaanti) ho aap par ai nabee, aur allaah kee rahamaten aur unakee barakaten. aman (shaanti) ho ham par aur allaah ke nek ibaadatagujaar bandon par. main gavaahee deta ya detee hoon ki allaah ke siva koee bhee ibaadat ke laayak nahee, aur main gavaahee deta ya detee hoon ki muhammad (sallallaahu alaihi vasallam) unake bande or unake rasool (paigambar) hai.’

agar yah namaaz kee aakhiree rakat hai to is hisse ko tashahood mein jod dho:

"Allahumma salli ‘ala Muhammadin wa ‘ala aali Muhammad
Kamaa salayta ‘ala aali Ibraaheem wa ‘ala aali Ibrahim Innaka Hameedun Majeed

Wa baarik ‘ala Muhammadin wa ‘ala aali Muhammad
Kamaa baarakta ‘ala Ibraaheem wa ‘ala aali Ibrahim Innaka Hameedun Majeed"

meaning:‘Eh Allah rahmat Nazil farma Muhammad par aur Aal-e Muhammad par jaisey tuney Rahmat Nazil farmayee Ibrahim (Alaihi as Salam) par aur Aal-e Ibrahim (Alaihi as Salam) par yakeenan tu Qabil-e-Tarif aur badi Shaan wala hai. Eh Allah Barkat Nazil farma Muhammad par aur Aal-e Muhammad par jaisey tuney Barkat Nazil farmayee Ibrahim (Alaihi as Salam) par aur Aal-e Ibrahim (Alaihi as Salam) par yakeenan tu Qabil-e-Tarif aur badi Shaan wala hai.. ’
