ALAM-E-ISLAM
SURAH AL-MASAD (సూరా అల్-మసాద్)
  • بِسْمِ اللهِ الرَّحْمنِ الرَّحِيمِِ
  • Shuru kaarta hu allah ke naam se jo rehmaan aur raheem hai

  • قُلْ يَـٰٓأَيُّهَا ٱلْكَـٰفِرُونَ
  • (he nabee!) kah doh hey kaafiro!

  • لَآ أَعْبُدُ مَا تَعْبُدُونَ
  • may un (moortiyon) ki ibadat nahi karta hu , jinhen tum ibadat karte ho.

  • وَلَآ أَنتُمْ عَـٰبِدُونَ مَآ أَعْبُدُ
  • aur na tum uski ibadat karte ho, jiski my ibadat karta hu.

  • وَلَآ أَنَا۠ عَابِدٌۭ مَّا عَبَدتُّمْ
  • aur na my uski ibadat karunga, jiski tum ibadat karte ho.

  • وَلَآ أَنتُمْ عَـٰبِدُونَ مَآ أَعْبُدُ
  • aur na tum uski ibadat karoge, jiski my ibaadat karta hu.

  • لَكُمْ دِينُكُمْ وَلِىَ دِينِ
  • tho tumhaare liye tumhaara deen aur mere liye mera deen

  • Bismillaahir Rahmaanir Raheem
  • In the name of Allah, Most Gracious, Most Merciful.

  • 1.Qul yaaa-ayyuhal kaafiroon
  • Say, ˹O Prophet,˺ “O you disbelievers!

  • 2.Laaa a'budu maa t'abudoon
  • I do not worship what you worship,

  • 3.Wa laaa antum 'aabidoona maaa a'bud
  • nor do you worship what I worship.

  • 4.Wa laaa ana 'abidum maa 'abattum
  • I will never worship what you worship,

  • 5.Wa laaa antum 'aabidoona maaa a'bud
  • nor will you ever worship what I worship.


  • 6.Lakum deenukum wa liya deen.
  • You have your way, and I have my Way.”.
