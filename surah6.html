ALAM-E-ISLAM
SURAH -AL-QADR (సూరా అల్-ఖద్ర్)
  • بِسْمِ اللهِ الرَّحْمنِ الرَّحِيمِِ
  • Shuru kaarta hu allah ke naam se jo rehmaan aur raheem hai

  • إِنَّآ أَنزَلْنَـٰهُ فِى لَيْلَةِ ٱلْقَدْرِ
  • Beshak hum ne Quran ko shabe kadra me nazil farmaya hai

  • وَمَآ أَدْرَىٰكَ مَا لَيْلَةُ ٱلْقَدْرِ
  • aur apko malum hai ki shabe-e-qadr kya hai ?

  • لَيْلَةُ ٱلْقَدْرِ خَيْرٌۭ مِّنْ أَلْفِ شَهْرٍۢ
  • shab-e-qadr hazaro mahine so behtar hai

  • تَنَزَّلُ ٱلْمَلَـٰٓئِكَةُ وَٱلرُّوحُ فِيهَا بِإِذْنِ رَبِّهِم مِّن كُلِّ أَمْرٍۢ
  • is raat mein farishte roohul ameen (jibareel alaihis salaam) apane rab ke har kaam ka hukm lekar utarate hain

  • سَلَـٰمٌ هِىَ حَتَّىٰ مَطْلَعِ ٱلْفَجْرِ
  • ye raat pooree tarah salaamatee hai, jo subah fajr hone tak rahatee hai.


  • Bismillaahir Rahmaanir Raheem
  • In the name of Allah, Most Gracious, Most Merciful.

  • 1.Innaa anzalnaahu fee lailatil qadr
  • Indeed, ˹it is˺ We ˹Who˺ sent this ˹Quran˺ down on the Night of Glory

  • 2.Wa maa adraaka ma lailatul qadr
  • And what will make you realize what the Night of Glory is?

  • 3.Lailatul qadri khairum min alfee shahr
  • The Night of Glory is better than a thousand months.

  • 4.Tanaz zalul malaa-ikatu war roohu feeha bi izni-rab bihim min kulli amr
  • That night the angels and the ˹holy˺ spirit1 descend, by the permission of their Lord, for every ˹decreed˺ matter.

  • 5. Salaamun hiya hattaa mat la'il fajr
  • It is all peace until the break of dawn.
